About Eng. Moh’d Kamal

Eng. Moh’d Kamal is a multidisciplinary Civil Engineer with over 34 years of experience in managing complex construction projects exceeding USD 20 billion across the Middle East, Gulf, and Africa. He specializes in Contracts (FIDIC, NEC, JCT), Project Controls, Procurement, Cost & Budgeting, Tendering, Claims, and Dispute Resolution.

He is highly skilled in full project lifecycle management, commercial strategy, facility management, claims management (preparation and analysis), forensic delay analysis, and alternative dispute resolution (ADR).
