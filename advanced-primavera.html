Advanced Planning Using Primavera P6

This advanced course is designed for professionals who already have a basic understanding of Primavera P6 and want to enhance their skills in managing complex projects. It focuses on advanced scheduling, earned value analysis, resource optimization, and multi-project management.

Course Objectives

  • Master advanced features and techniques of Primavera P6.
  • Manage multiple projects and complex dependencies efficiently.
  • Apply resource leveling, optimization, and earned value management.
  • Generate professional reports and dashboards for project tracking.

Topics Covered

  • Advanced Scheduling Techniques
  • Global Change and Custom Fields
  • Resource Optimization and Leveling
  • Earned Value Management and Performance Tracking
  • Multi-Project Management and Portfolio Analysis
  • Advanced Reporting and Dashboards

Course Details

  • Duration: 24 Hours
  • Level: Advanced
  • Language: English
  • Instructor: Eng. Moh’d Kamal
