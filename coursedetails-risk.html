Risk Management & Project Control

This course provides a comprehensive understanding of identifying, assessing, and controlling project risks. It emphasizes practical tools and methodologies for risk management, cost control, and schedule monitoring to ensure project success.

Course Objectives

  • Identify and evaluate risks that may impact project objectives.
  • Develop risk response strategies and mitigation plans.
  • Apply tools for cost and schedule control in project execution.
  • Enhance decision-making using quantitative risk analysis techniques.

Topics Covered

  • Introduction to Risk Management Concepts
  • Risk Identification and Assessment Methods
  • Qualitative & Quantitative Risk Analysis
  • Risk Response Planning and Monitoring
  • Project Cost & Schedule Control Techniques
  • Performance Measurement (EVM – Earned Value Management)

Course Details

  • Duration: 20 Hours
  • Level: Intermediate
  • Language: English
  • Instructor: Eng. Moh’d Kamal

Course Breakdown

Introduction to project risk management framework, definitions, and risk categories.

Techniques for identifying risks and assessing their probability and impact.

Applying cost and schedule control, EVM, and performance analysis for ongoing projects.
