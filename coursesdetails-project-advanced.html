Project Management (Advanced)

This course builds on fundamental project management concepts and introduces advanced tools, techniques, and leadership skills needed to manage complex projects successfully. It covers strategic project planning, stakeholder management, and performance optimization.

Course Objectives

  • Apply advanced project management methodologies and frameworks.
  • Enhance leadership and stakeholder communication skills.
  • Use advanced tools for cost, schedule, and quality control.
  • Understand risk, performance, and strategic project alignment.

Topics Covered

  • Strategic Project Planning & Portfolio Management
  • Advanced Scheduling & Resource Management
  • Leadership, Communication & Conflict Resolution
  • Performance Monitoring & Control
  • Risk and Change Management Integration
  • Agile & Hybrid Project Management Approaches

Course Details

  • Duration: 24 Hours
  • Level: Advanced
  • Language: English
  • Instructor: Eng. Moh’d Kamal
