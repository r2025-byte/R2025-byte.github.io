Claims Management & Dispute Resolution

This course provides professionals with essential knowledge and tools to effectively identify, prepare, and manage contractual claims. It also covers methods of dispute avoidance and resolution under international contract standards, including FIDIC principles.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the nature and causes of contractual claims.
  • Learn claim identification, notification, and documentation techniques.
  • Gain knowledge of dispute resolution mechanisms and procedures.
  • Apply best practices for negotiation and claim settlement.

Topics Covered

  • Types and Causes of Claims
  • Claim Identification and Documentation
  • Claim Evaluation and Submission
  • FIDIC Clauses Related to Claims
  • Dispute Avoidance and Negotiation Techniques
  • Dispute Resolution Methods: ADR, Arbitration, Litigation

Course Details

  • Duration: 20 Hours
  • Level: Intermediate to Advanced
  • Language: English
  • Instructor: Eng. Moh’d Kamal

Course Breakdown

Overview of claim types, causes, and contractual implications in construction projects.
