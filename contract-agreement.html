Contract Agreement & Administration

This course provides a comprehensive overview of contract agreements, administration procedures, and effective management practices. Participants will learn how to interpret contract clauses, manage changes, and ensure successful contract execution.

Course Objectives

  • Understand key principles and clauses in contract agreements.
  • Develop practical knowledge of contract administration procedures.
  • Learn how to manage variations, value engineering, and documentation.
  • Gain insight into roles and responsibilities within contract management.

Topics Covered

  • Agreement & Contract Information
  • Variation Clause and Force Majeure
  • Priority of Contract Documents
  • Termination by Contractor
  • Contracts Administration – Key Areas & Actions
  • Valuation of Changes
  • Value Engineering

Course Details

  • Duration: 20 Hours
  • Level: Intermediate
  • Language: English
  • Instructor: Eng. Moh’d Kamal
