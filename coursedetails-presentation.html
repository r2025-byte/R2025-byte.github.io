Presentation & Communication Skills

This course helps participants develop strong communication and presentation skills essential for professional success. It focuses on verbal, non-verbal, and written communication, as well as effective presentation delivery and audience engagement.

Course Objectives

  • Enhance verbal and non-verbal communication skills.
  • Develop confidence in public speaking and presentation delivery.
  • Learn techniques to engage, persuade, and inspire audiences.
  • Improve clarity, tone, and structure of professional communication.

Topics Covered

  • Fundamentals of Effective Communication
  • Verbal & Non-Verbal Communication Techniques
  • Presentation Structure and Storytelling
  • Visual Aids and Slide Design Principles
  • Public Speaking Confidence and Body Language
  • Handling Questions and Audience Interaction

Course Details

  • Duration: 12 Hours
  • Level: General / All Professionals
  • Language: English
  • Instructor: Eng. Moh’d Kamal
