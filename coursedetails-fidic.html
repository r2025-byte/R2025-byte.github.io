FIDIC Contracts

This course introduces participants to the structure, key provisions, and practical applications of FIDIC contracts. It provides a detailed understanding of roles, responsibilities, and risk allocation under the different FIDIC contract types.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the structure and main clauses of FIDIC contracts.
  • Distinguish between different FIDIC books and their applications.
  • Identify roles and obligations of employers, engineers, and contractors.
  • Interpret key contractual provisions such as variations, claims, and payments.

Topics Covered

  • Overview of FIDIC Contract Series (Red, Yellow, Silver, Green Books)
  • Roles and Responsibilities of Contracting Parties
  • Key Clauses: Variations, Claims, Payments, and Termination
  • FIDIC Risk Allocation Principles
  • Contract Administration and Dispute Resolution Mechanisms

Course Details

  • Duration: 20 Hours
  • Level: Intermediate to Advanced
  • Language: English
  • Instructor: Eng. Moh’d Kamal
