Contract Definition & Contract Management

This course introduces the principles and practices of contract definition and management. It provides professionals with the knowledge needed to understand contract structures, stakeholders, and management processes throughout the contract life cycle.

Course Objectives

  • Understand what makes a contract legally valid.
  • Recognize different types and forms of contracts.
  • Identify stakeholders and their roles in the contract life cycle.
  • Develop essential contract management skills and competencies.

Topics Covered

  • Requirements for a Legally Valid Contract
  • Contract Terminologies and Categories
  • Contents and Forms of Contracts
  • Contract Management Profession & Competencies
  • Contract Life Cycle & Stakeholders

Course Details

  • Duration: 16 Hours
  • Level: Foundation
  • Language: English
  • Instructor: Eng. Moh’d Kamal
