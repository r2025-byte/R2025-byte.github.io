Procurement & Supply Chain Management (Advanced)

This course is designed for professionals aiming to master advanced concepts in procurement and supply chain management. It covers strategic sourcing, supplier evaluation, risk management, and performance optimization within modern global supply chains.

Course Objectives

  • Understand advanced procurement and supply chain processes.
  • Develop strategies for supplier relationship and performance management.
  • Learn contract negotiation and sourcing optimization techniques.
  • Apply risk analysis and mitigation strategies in procurement operations.

Topics Covered

  • Procurement Processes & Life Cycle
  • Strategic Sourcing and Supplier Selection
  • Supplier Evaluation & Performance Metrics
  • Negotiation and Cost Analysis
  • Risk Management in Procurement
  • Supply Chain Integration & Digitalization
  • Ethical and Sustainable Procurement Practices

Course Details

  • Duration: 20 Hours
  • Level: Advanced
  • Language: English
  • Instructor: Eng. Moh’d Kamal
