Acquisition & Contract Competencies

This course focuses on developing professional competencies in acquisition planning, contracting strategies, and contract lifecycle management. It helps participants understand the pre-award, award, and post-award phases of contracting and equips them to make informed decisions in procurement and sourcing processes.

Course Objectives

  • Understand acquisition planning and market research principles.
  • Learn effective contracting methods and selection techniques.
  • Manage all stages of contract development from pre-award to post-award.
  • Enhance general business and management competencies.

Topics Covered

  • Acquisition Planning and Market Research (Internal & External)
  • Contracting Methods and Strategies
  • Pre-Award Contract Competencies (Requirements, Negotiations, Evaluation)
  • Post-Award Contract Competencies
  • General Business Competencies

Course Details

  • Duration: 18 Hours
  • Level: Intermediate
  • Language: English
  • Instructor: Eng. Moh’d Kamal
