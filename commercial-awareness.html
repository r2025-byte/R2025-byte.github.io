Commercial Awareness & Supply Chain Management

This course enhances participants’ understanding of commercial relationships, competition laws, and the principles of supply chain management. It focuses on building awareness of business operations and supply chain efficiency across different industries.

Course Objectives

  • Understand key principles of commercial awareness and relationships.
  • Gain insight into competition law, partnerships, and joint ventures.
  • Develop knowledge in managing and improving supply chain performance.
  • Promote ethical and sustainable practices in procurement and sourcing.

Topics Covered

  • Key Commercial Terms & Relationships
  • Competition Law and Market Practices
  • Partnerships, Consortia, and Joint Ventures
  • Supply Chain Management Principles
  • Supply Chain Design, Metrics, and SCOR Models
  • Green Procurement & Code of Ethics

Course Details

  • Duration: 16 Hours
  • Level: Intermediate
  • Language: English
  • Instructor: Eng. Moh’d Kamal
