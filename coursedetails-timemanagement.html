Time Management & Productivity

This course focuses on improving personal and professional productivity through effective time management techniques. Participants will learn how to prioritize tasks, overcome procrastination, and achieve better work-life balance.

Course Objectives

  • Develop practical time management and organization skills.
  • Understand how to set SMART goals and priorities effectively.
  • Improve productivity and focus through proven techniques.
  • Enhance decision-making and stress management under pressure.

Topics Covered

  • Understanding Time Management Principles
  • Setting Goals & Priorities (SMART Framework)
  • Task Delegation and Scheduling
  • Dealing with Procrastination
  • Productivity Tools & Techniques
  • Stress & Workload Management

Course Details

  • Duration: 12 Hours
  • Level: General / All Professionals
  • Language: English
  • Instructor: Eng. Moh’d Kamal

Course Breakdown

Introduction to time management concepts, identifying time wasters, and self-assessment.

How to apply the SMART goal framework and set daily, weekly, and long-term priorities.

Using scheduling tools, delegation, and techniques to maintain focus and reduce distractions.

Managing workload pressure, reducing stress, and maintaining a healthy work-life balance.
